Bring Me the Horizon are a British rock band, formed in Sheffield in 2004. The group currently consists of lead vocalist Oli Sykes, guitarist Lee Malia, bassist Matt Kean, and drummer Matt Nicholls.

The band released their debut album Count Your Blessings in 2006. Upon release, the album's deathcore sound polarised listeners, and was largely met with critical disdain.

Bring Me the Horizon's founding members came from diverse musical backgrounds. Matt Nicholls and Oliver Sykes had a common interest in American metalcore.
